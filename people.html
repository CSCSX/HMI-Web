Linfeng Zhang  |  Ph.D Candidate

Linfeng Zhang comes from Tsinghua University, Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS). He gets his Bachelor degree in Northeastern University, School of Computer Science in 2019. Currently He is a Phd candidate supervised by Kaisheng Ma. His major research interest is to improve the effectiveness and efficiency of deep neural networks via knowledge distillation. In 2019, he firstly proposes the idea of self-distillation. So far, he has also successfully applied knowledge distillation to all kinds of tasks, such as 2D classification detection, segmentation, image-to-image translation, video recognition, point cloud-based detection, BEV based multi-view detection, representation and so on. Please refer to his homepage for more information.
